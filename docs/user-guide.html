MKJM Bookstore
User Guide

A complete step-by-step guide to using the MKJM Bookstore at www.mkjm.ca. This guide covers everything from creating your account to browsing books and placing orders.

Site Overview

MKJM Bookstore is an online bookstore where you can browse a curated collection of books, add them to your cart, and place orders. The site also includes a Wiki section with reading guides, genre breakdowns, and book-to-movie adaptations.

Navigation Bar

The navigation bar is visible at the top of every page and contains the following links:

🏠

Home

Returns to the main homepage with featured books and site sections.

🔍

Search

Jump directly to the book search bar.

📖

Wiki

Browse reading guides, genres, and book-to-movie info.

🛒

Cart

View items you've added and proceed to checkout.

👤

Your Name

Access your account settings and order history.

Tip: If you are not logged in, the navigation will show a Login link instead of your name. You must be logged in to add books to your cart or place orders.

Creating an Account

signup.php

To use the full features of MKJM Bookstore — including adding to cart and placing orders — you need to create a free account.

Step-by-Step

  1. 1Click the Login link in the top navigation bar.
  2. 2On the login page, click Sign Up at the bottom of the form.
  3. 3Fill in your desired Username, Email address, Password, and Confirm Password.
  4. 4Click Create Account.
  5. 5You will be automatically logged in and redirected to the homepage.

Requirements

FieldRequirement
UsernameMust be unique — no two accounts can share a username.
EmailMust be a valid email address and not already registered.
PasswordMinimum 6 characters.
Confirm PasswordMust exactly match the password field.
Note: If your chosen username or email is already in use, you will see an error message and can try a different one.

Logging In

login.php

If you already have an account, log in to access your cart, orders, and account settings.

  1. 1Click Login in the top navigation bar.
  2. 2Enter your Username and Password.
  3. 3Click Login. You will be redirected to the homepage.
Forgot your password? Contact the site administrator to have your password reset.

Browsing Books

index.php / book.php

The homepage is the main way to discover books on the site. It is divided into several sections:

Featured Books

When you first arrive at the homepage, you will see a curated selection of featured books displayed as cards. Each card shows the book's cover image, title, author, price, and star rating. Click on any book card to view its full details page.

Book Detail Page

Clicking a book takes you to its detail page, which shows:

  • →Full title and author name
  • →Price and current stock availability
  • →Star rating (0–5)
  • →Cover image
  • →Format selection (Paperback or Hardcover) and Add to Cart button
Note: You must be logged in to add a book to your cart. If you are not logged in, you will be redirected to the login page.

Searching for Books

search.php

Use the search bar in the navigation to find a specific book quickly.

  1. 1Click the search icon or the Search link in the navigation.
  2. 2Type a book title or author name into the search box.
  3. 3Matching results will appear as you type or after you press Enter.
  4. 4Click any result to go to that book's detail page.

Your Cart

cart.php

Your cart holds all the books you've selected before placing an order.

Adding a Book

  1. 1Navigate to a book's detail page.
  2. 2Select a format — Paperback or Hardcover.
  3. 3Click Add to Cart.
  4. 4The cart icon in the navigation will update to reflect your new item.

Viewing Your Cart

  1. 1Click the Cart icon in the navigation bar.
  2. 2You will see all items currently in your cart, including title, format, quantity, and price.
  3. 3You can remove items from your cart on this page.
Note: Each unique combination of book and format (e.g. Dune — Hardcover) is treated as a separate cart item.

Checkout

process_order.php

Once you are happy with your cart, you can place your order.

  1. 1Go to your cart by clicking Cart in the navigation.
  2. 2Review your items and confirm everything looks correct.
  3. 3Click Proceed to Checkout.
  4. 4Fill in your shipping and payment details on the checkout form.
  5. 5Submit your order. You will receive a confirmation on screen.
Note: Online payment and shipping processing is currently being finalized. Orders submitted now will be reviewed and confirmed by the store administrator.

Account Settings

account.php

You can manage your personal information by clicking your username in the top navigation bar.

What You Can Do

  • →Edit your name — Update the display name shown in the navigation.
  • →Change your password — Enter a new password to update your login credentials.
  • →View your cart — Quick link to your current cart.
  • →Logout — Sign out of your account.

Changing Your Password

  1. 1Go to your account page by clicking your name in the navigation.
  2. 2Find the Change Password section.
  3. 3Enter your new password and confirm it.
  4. 4Click Save Changes.

Order History

Order history tracking is currently being implemented. Once available, you will be able to view a full list of your past orders — including order dates, items purchased, formats, quantities, and totals — directly from your account page.

Coming soon: Full order tracking will be available in an upcoming update to the site.

Logging Out

logout.php

To log out of your account:

  1. 1Click your username in the top navigation bar.
  2. 2Click Logout.
  3. 3You will be redirected to the homepage as a guest.
Security tip: Always log out when using a shared or public computer.
